Pricing

Scales with your practice.

You pay for the clients you have. Not the seats you might fill. No feature gates. No multi-year lock-ins. One annual contract, billed monthly, priced entirely by your active client roster.

Client-based pricing

One price per active client slot. Covers everything the platform does for that client across the entire year. A boutique firm with five clients pays for five. A large practice with fifty pays for fifty. The model works at every scale.
